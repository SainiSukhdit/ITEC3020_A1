Welcome to my favourite weekend vacation destination page. Upon, analyzing between multiple holiday options, I concluded that Tobermory should be a prime vacation for weekends. Tobermory is a habour village located on Brunce Peninsula, Ontario. The name "Tobermory" is derived from the gaelic language which means the "Well of Mary". The water from Mary's well was said to possess medicinal properties. I believe this a prime location for a vacation since there are a wide range of options for activities.

The best way to get to Tobermory is from Toronto. You can either use a bus or a car. The cheapest way to get from Toronto to Tobermory is to drive which costs $55 - $85 and takes 4h 16m. There is a direct bus departing from Toronto at 34, Asquith Ave and arriving at Tobermory at Escarpment Heights Motel. Services depart every 30 minutes. The journey takes approximately 5h 15m. Toronto to Tobermory bus services is operated by Parkbus starting from Tobermory at Escarpment Heights Motel station.
